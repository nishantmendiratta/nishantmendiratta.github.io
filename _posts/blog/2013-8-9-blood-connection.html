The first "BC" android application...!!!

Hello,
This application is based on an idea of getting information of all blood donors across the nation .
The main motive is to create a link between the donors and the receivers.
This is just a beginning , If you are interested in donating blood to needy ones then register your self with bloodconnection .
Your information will not be secured as this is the only information which will help the needy ones.

Our Facebook Application http://apps.facebook.com/bloodconnection is also available for those who don't have an android phone.
